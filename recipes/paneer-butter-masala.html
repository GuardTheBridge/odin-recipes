Paneer Butter Masala

Description

This is one of the premium North Indian or Punjabi curries.

It is known for its mildly sweet and spicy taste with the goodness of fresh cream and butter topping with moist paneer.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 cup Paneer cubes
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tbsp Oil
  • 2 cubes Butter
  • 1 Black cardamom
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 5 Green cardamom
  • 1 tsp each of Ginger and Garlic
  • 1 cup each of Onion and Tomato
  • 7-8 nos Cashews
  • 1/4 tsp Green chilli
  • Oil, Butter, Salt as required
  • 1/2 tsp each of Red chilli powder, Coriander and Cumin powder, Garam Masala
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Kasoori methi
  • 1/2 tsp Honey
  • 3/4 cup Milk
  • 1 tbsp Cream
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom powder

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Put paneer cubes to it. Add salt and red chilli powder to it.
  3. Pan fry the paneer cubes.
  4. Heat oil in a pan again. Add butter.
  5. Now add black cardamom, cinnamon and green cardamom.
  6. Stir a little and then add ginger and garlic. Mix well.
  7. Put chopped onion and tomato. Mix again.
  8. Add cashews and green chilli to the mix and puree the mixture.
  9. Now heat oil in another pan. Add butter.
  10. Once the butter starts to flutter, add the pureed mixture to the pan.Mix and let it simmer.
  11. Add salt, red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala and black pepper. Mix well.
  12. Keep stirring and let the gravy simmer and add kasoori methi followed by honey.
  13. Add fried paneer cubes to the simmering gravy. Mix well.
  14. Now add milk and mix thoroughly.
  15. Let it come to simmer and then add cream along with cardamom powder. Mix well.
  16. Serve hot.
